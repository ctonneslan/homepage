About Me

Hey there! I’m a front-end web developer with a passion for clean design, responsive layouts, and pixel-perfect code. My journey started with curiosity and late-night YouTube tutorials, and now I’m building full-featured websites with accessibility, performance, and great UX in mind.

When I’m not coding, I’m likely exploring music production, playing chess, or hiking around Pennsylvania. I believe in lifelong learning, building real things, and helping others break into tech. This homepage is both a playground and a reflection of everything I’ve been practicing.
